# Good Evening, Burlington!

Burlington’s forecast sees early-week precipitation transitioning from rain to snow, with slick surfaces likely during commute times. Another wave of wintry weather could arrive by Thursday, so keep those windshield scrapers close and plan for changing conditions.

If you’ve stepped outside lately, you’ll notice Burlington can’t quite decide whether to soak us or bury us in snow. One moment it’s a slushy drizzle, the next it’s chunky flurries floating by—classic February mood swings, so buckle up until midweek when we catch our breath (briefly) before more flakes and chill return.

Speaking of midweek, you won’t lack for things to do around town if you’re feeling adventurous between snow squalls. Pop by Monday’s open mics or trivia nights in Winooski, swing your partner at the Champlain Club on Tuesday, then catch local jazz, comedy, or even a cozy round of chair yoga Wednesday—just keep an eye on those slick sidewalks and stay caffeinated, folks!

# [Co-founder of Burlington-based company focused on breastfeeding to take over as CEO](https://vermontbiz.com/news/2025/january/27/christine-dodson-named-new-ceo-mamava)
